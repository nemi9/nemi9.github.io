About Lady Xavier

Xavier Bost, affectionately known as Lady X, is a Brooklyn girl who’s sincere love for God, her community, Arts/Entertainment and Animal Activism, has led her on an incredible journey over the past 13years. Xavier is a multifaceted performer & entertainer who’s not only a strong Soprano singer, but also a flourishing actor, media personality & entrepreneur, writer, dancer, activist and so much more. She enjoys using her gifts and passions to positively touch the community, emotionally connect with her audience, and show that anything is possible once you have Christ in your life! Her true loves are music and acting. But little did she know God gave her another gift...the gift of Media Hosting and Producing.

In May of 2010 she was presented an incredible opportunity that led to Xavier co-founding the O.D.X-treme Praise Internet Radio Show. She later became the staple host and changed the name to the X-treme Praise Show. To date guest of the show featured Panelist discussions on an array of Hot Topics that targets interests of a diverse audience, both in and outside the "Church". Lady X and the team have interviewed media personalities, political figures, community member and Artist.

Through this new media platform she’s not only shared and connected people with knowledge and information to help them in both their spiritual and every day lives, but she’s organized & hosted several charitable events such as the Xtreme Praise Show annual Xtreme Giving Coat Drive concert where collected coat, toy & shoe donations to aid numerous organizations efforts in helping others across both the US and internationally.
