April Walker

ENTREPRENEUR / PROPRIETOR OF WALKER WEAR / A.WALKER GROUP

April Walker, a pioneer and savvy businesswoman, in the urban fashion world set the stage for many of the designers and brands to come after her brand. Walker Wear has inspired a fashion lifestyle category that commands billions annually. As a designer, Walker spawned the company “Walker Wear” in the early ‘90’s. One of the first to identify the value of product placement, with celebrity endorsements from the likes of Tupac, BIG, and even Beavis and Butt-Head, her brand changed the game landing accounts such as Macy’s, Footlocker, Spiegel, Finish Line, Dayton’s, and Dillard’s. Recently, there has been a resurgence of Walker Wear in fashion.

April claims brooklyn, but her journey originally started from L.A. as a student of New Paltz University, she studied communications and business. A. Walker has also worked as a VP at Phat farm, managing the growth and all of the lecensees. she implemented a women’s division for And-1 from “mind to marketplace”. she’s consulted for brands such as Ecko, Champion, Russell Athletics, Vivian Kay, IDG, Money clothing, and business outreach center network, a micro-enterprise and business development organization.
